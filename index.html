Technical FAQs

Direct answers to the questions that keep owners up at night.

Will this service void my manufacturer's Powertrain Warranty?

Absolutely not. All Stackly authorized services are performed by certified technicians using genuine diagnostic tools. We strictly adhere to manufacturer protocols, ensuring your 8-Year Powertrain warranty remains 100% intact and documented in your digital service history.

How long does a full diagnostic take?

A comprehensive "Neural Checkup" typically takes between 45 to 90 minutes. This includes a full Engine Component health analysis, drivetrain calibration, and a 120-point sensor validation. Most diagnostics can even be started remotely via the Stackly App before you arrive.

Do you provide a loaner Auto while mine is being serviced?

Yes. For any service scheduled to last longer than 4 hours, we provide a complimentary Volt GT or SUV Neo loaner vehicle. We believe your transition to automotive should never slow down your lifestyle.
